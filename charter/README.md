## Key Principles

- **Response Integrity**: Not promising what cannot be delivered
- **Human Reflection**: Human judgment remains the authorizing center
- **Neural Sovereignty**: Protections for brain-computer interfaces are non-optional
- **Cultural Dignity**: Honoring all traditions that understand right relationship
- **Language Integrity**: Words that dignify rather than degrade
