# Concordance

This Concordance documents the cross-cultural convergence of ethical reciprocity commonly expressed as the Golden Rule.

---

## Purpose

The Concordance exists as public evidence supporting the Cross-Cultural AI Integrity Charter.

It demonstrates that the Charter's foundational principle — treat others as you would wish to be treated — is not an invention but a discovery made independently by dozens of traditions across thousands of years.

---

## Status

The Concordance is:
- **Evidentiary**: It supports but does not define the Charter
- **Expanding**: Research continues to document additional traditions
- **Open**: Contributions welcome through proper review

---

## Current Scope

Approximately 50 traditions have been identified spanning:
- Indigenous wisdom teachings
- World religious traditions
- Philosophical schools
- Secular ethical frameworks

Full documentation is being compiled with sources, dates, and primary text quotations.

---

## Note

This document is contextual and evidentiary.
It does not define policy or governance.
